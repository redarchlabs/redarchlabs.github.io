Role-based security

Every answer respects who's asking.

Role-based access is the heart of Red Arch, not a plugin. Grant access by region, department, role, and group, and it holds everywhere — documents, records, search, and AI chat all obey the same rules. Multi-tenant isolation keeps organizations fully separated at the database level.
